About Us

Since its establishment in 1994, Le Potager has earned a distinguished reputation for delivering premium natural products. As a family-owned business based in Lebanon, Le Potager initially specialized in the production and distribution of high-quality olive oil.

Throughout the years, our enterprise has undergone a transformative evolution. Presently, Le Potager proudly offers a diverse portfolio of 35 meticulously crafted products, encompassing cold-pressed 100% natural virgin olive oil sourced from Koura, North Lebanon, an assortment of herbs, and exquisite syrups.

Additionally, our commitment to health and sustainability is exemplified through Le Potager Bio, our initiative that involves cultivating and delivering over 30 varieties of 100% organic vegetables directly to our customer's doorsteps.

Zeina Daoud

Meet Zeina, a pioneering entrepreneur who wears many hats with grace and determination. As the visionary founder and CEO of Le Potager, Zeina has cultivated not only a thriving business but also a legacy of empowerment. Her remarkable achievements were recognized in 2016 when she was honored as Women Entrepreneur of the Year, a testament to her leadership and innovative spirit.

Beyond the boardroom, Zeina's greatest source of pride is her role as a loving mother to three incredible children. Her journey exemplifies the harmonious blend of professional success and personal fulfillment, making Zeina an inspiring figure in both the business world and family life.
